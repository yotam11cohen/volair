About VolAir

Our story, our mission, and the values that guide every flight

Who We Are

VolAir Airlines was founded with a single, bold conviction: that flying between Israel and Europe should be affordable, comfortable, and joyful — without compromise.

Since our first flight in 2018, we have grown from a small regional carrier into one of the most trusted airlines connecting Tel Aviv to the most beautiful cities in Europe. Today, we operate direct routes to Barcelona, Lisbon, Rome, and Prague — with more destinations on the horizon.

We are not just an airline. We are a bridge between cultures, a gateway to adventure, and a commitment to bringing people closer together — one flight at a time.

Our Mission

To make European travel accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable for every passenger — by combining competitive fares, modern aircraft, exceptional crew, and a deep respect for the planet and the communities we serve.

Our Journey

2018

VolAir Takes Flight

Founded in Tel Aviv with two leased Airbus A320s and a team of 45 passionate aviation professionals. First route: Tel Aviv ↔ Barcelona.

2019

Rome & Lisbon Routes Launched

Rapid growth allowed us to add two new European destinations and expand our fleet to five aircraft.

2020

Navigating Challenges

Like the entire aviation industry, we faced unprecedented disruption. We used the pause to invest in our fleet, technology, and people.

2021

Return & Rebound

Resumed full operations with enhanced health protocols. Welcomed back passengers who had missed the freedom of travel.

2022

Prague Route & A321 Fleet Upgrade

Added Prague to our network and took delivery of our first Airbus A321, increasing capacity and passenger comfort significantly.

2023

500,000 Passengers Milestone

Celebrated carrying our 500,000th passenger — a milestone that reflects the trust our customers place in us every day.

2024

A320neo Green Fleet Initiative

Introduced our first A320neo aircraft as part of our long-term sustainability commitment, reducing emissions on key routes.

2026

Looking Ahead

Expanding our digital booking experience, exploring new routes, and doubling down on our commitment to sustainable aviation.
