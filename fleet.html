A321 — The Workhorse

The Airbus A321 is the backbone of the VolAir fleet. With its stretched fuselage and advanced CFM LEAP-1A engines, it delivers exceptional range and efficiency on our medium-haul European routes.

Passengers enjoy a wide, airy cabin with overhead storage, mood lighting, and our signature VolAir in-seat entertainment system on every seat.

A320neo — The Eco Star

The A320neo (New Engine Option) represents the future of sustainable aviation. With 20% lower fuel consumption and dramatically reduced noise emissions, it is our greenest aircraft — perfect for short-to-medium haul routes.

Its sharklet wingtip devices and LEAP-1A engines make every flight kinder to the planet, without compromising a single degree of passenger comfort.

Our Commitment to Sustainable Aviation

VolAir Airlines is committed to reducing its carbon footprint year on year. By operating the latest-generation Airbus aircraft, investing in Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF), and optimising our flight paths, we aim to achieve net-zero emissions by 2040.
